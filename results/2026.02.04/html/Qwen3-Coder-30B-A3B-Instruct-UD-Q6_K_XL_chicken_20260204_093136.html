Happy Mrs. Chicken Simulation

Simulation Implementation Notes

This Happy Mrs. Chicken simulation features:

  • Chicken Character with dynamic movement and state management
  • Wandering AI that changes direction randomly and bounces off edges
  • Egg Laying Mechanics that occur periodically with visual cues
  • State Management for both chicken (wandering/laying) and eggs
  • Frame-rate Independent Animation using delta time
  • Visual Details including directional orientation, egg appearance, and fading effect

Key design decisions:

  • The chicken changes direction occasionally to create natural wandering behavior
  • Egg laying is triggered by a random probability to make it feel organic
  • Chicken turns orange when laying to provide visual feedback
  • Eggs fade out over time to simulate them being collected
  • Background elements (grass, clouds) enhance the visual appeal
