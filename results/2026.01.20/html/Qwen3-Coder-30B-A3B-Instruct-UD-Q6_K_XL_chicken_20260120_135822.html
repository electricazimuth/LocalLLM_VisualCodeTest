Happy Mrs. Chicken Simulation

Implementation Notes

This simulation features:

  • Chicken AI: Wandering behavior with periodic direction changes and boundary collision
  • Egg Laying: Chicken lays eggs periodically with a visual "laying" state
  • State Management: Chicken has states (wandering, laying) with smooth transitions
  • Delta Time: Frame-rate independent movement and timing
  • Visual Design: Chicken with beak and body that faces its direction of movement
  • Egg Physics: Eggs appear at chicken's position and remain static
  • Boundary Handling: Chicken stays within canvas bounds with realistic bouncing

Objects are managed with classes for clean code organization. The chicken's movement is based on velocity and direction, with random adjustments to create natural wandering behavior.
