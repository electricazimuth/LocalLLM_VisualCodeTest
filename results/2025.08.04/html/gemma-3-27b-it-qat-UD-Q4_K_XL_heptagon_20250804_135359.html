This code simulates bouncing balls in a spinning heptagon container using HTML Canvas and JavaScript.

Key features:

  • Heptagon Container: A spinning heptagon is drawn on the canvas.
  • Balls: 20 balls are created with random initial velocities and assigned colors and numbers.
  • Gravity: Balls are affected by gravity, causing them to fall.
  • Wall Collision: Balls bounce off the walls of the container with damping to simulate energy loss.
  • Ball-to-Ball Collision: Balls collide with each other, and their velocities are adjusted based on a simplified collision response.
  • Animation Loop: requestAnimationFrame is used for smooth animation.
