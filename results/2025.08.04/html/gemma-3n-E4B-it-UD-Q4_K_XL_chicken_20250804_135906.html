Developer notes regarding the simulation logic, AI choices, etc.

**Implementation Notes:**
  • Uses a simple circle for the chicken's body and a red circle for the beak.
  • Chicken's movement is a basic wandering AI with random direction changes.
  • Egg laying is triggered randomly with a 2% probability and a brief laying animation.
  • Uses `requestAnimationFrame` for smooth animation and delta time for frame-rate independence.
